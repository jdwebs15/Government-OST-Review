STATE-ALIGNED CIVIC ACTION PROCESS

        

          
1. Identify the issue
Define the real problem clearly.

          
2. Research
Gather evidence, facts, data, and rules.

          
3. Debate
Consider viewpoints, questions, and disagreements.

          
4. Propose appropriate solutions
Generate realistic policy or civic responses.

          
5. Devise action plans
Plan who does what, when, and how.

          
6. Examine possible consequences
Predict intended and unintended results.

          
7. Weigh costs and benefits
Judge which approach is most worthwhile.
